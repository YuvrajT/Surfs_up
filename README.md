# Surfs Up

## Overview:

The purpose of this analysis is to find out the temperatures in the month of June and Decemeber in Oahu, to determine if the surf and ice cream shop business is sustainable year around. 

### Results:

- From the results we can verify the min temp in june was 64 and in decemeber was 56. 
- Max temp in june was 85 and in Decemeber was 83
